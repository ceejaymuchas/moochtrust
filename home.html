About Us

MOOCH Foundation is a social welfare organization dedicated to improving the lives of children living in rural and marginalized communities of Zimbabwe, including areas such as Gokwe. We focus on providing food, school uniforms, sanitary wear, clean water, and safe educational infrastructure.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To uplift and empower children in rural Zimbabwe by providing essential resources and building sustainable community-driven solutions.

Our Vision

A Zimbabwe where every child has access to education, nutrition, clean water, and a safe environment to thrive.
