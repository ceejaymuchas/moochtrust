Mission & Vision

Mission Of Outreach for Children’s Hope (MOOCH) is committed to transforming the lives of children in rural Zimbabwe through sustainable social development.

Our Mission

The mission of MOOCH Foundation is to uplift, protect, and empower children living in rural and marginalized communities of Zimbabwe by addressing their most urgent needs while laying a strong foundation for long-term development. We are committed to providing access to nutritious food, quality education, adequate school uniforms, sanitary wear for girls, clean and safe drinking water, and safe learning environments through the construction and rehabilitation of schools.

MOOCH Foundation believes that child development is the cornerstone of national development. By supporting children at an early stage, particularly those in rural districts such as Gokwe, we aim to break cycles of poverty, school dropouts, preventable disease, and social exclusion. Our mission extends beyond relief; we focus on empowering communities to take ownership of projects, ensuring sustainability, accountability, and long-lasting impact.

Through partnerships with local schools, community leaders, development partners, and government institutions, MOOCH Foundation works in alignment with national priorities to strengthen education systems, improve child health, and promote inclusive rural development. Our work directly supports the national objective of creating empowered, educated, and healthy citizens capable of contributing meaningfully to Zimbabwe’s socio-economic growth.

Our Vision

Our vision is a Zimbabwe where every child, regardless of geographical location or socio-economic background, has equal access to education, nutrition, clean water, healthcare support, and a safe environment that enables them to reach their full potential. We envision rural communities where children attend school with dignity, confidence, and hope for a better future.

MOOCH Foundation envisions contributing to national development by nurturing a generation of informed, skilled, and socially responsible citizens. By investing in children today, we seek to support Zimbabwe’s long-term development agenda, including the national aspiration of achieving an upper middle-income economy through human capital development, inclusive growth, and social equity.

Our vision aligns strongly with government development frameworks that prioritize education, food security, water and sanitation, health, gender equality, and rural transformation. By focusing on children and community-driven solutions, MOOCH Foundation complements public sector efforts and helps bridge development gaps in hard-to-reach rural areas.

MOOCH Foundation remains committed to working collaboratively with government ministries, local authorities, traditional leaders, and development partners to ensure that its programs are aligned with national policies, sustainable development goals, and community priorities, ultimately contributing to a prosperous and inclusive Zimbabwe.
